Salesforce Integration

Connect to pull live pipeline data directly from your Salesforce org

Not Connected
Setup Guide (One-time, 5 min)
  1. In Salesforce, go to Setup → App Manager → New Connected App
  2. Fill in App Name: Sales Dashboard. Enable OAuth Settings, set callback URL to http://localhost
  3. Add scopes: Full access or api, refresh_token. Save and wait 2–10 min for it to activate.
  4. Copy the Consumer Key (Client ID) and Consumer Secret from the app. Paste them in the form →
  5. Get your Security Token from My Settings → Personal → Reset Security Token. Append it to your password in the form.
💡 What gets pulled
Opportunities (stage, amount, owner) · Activities (tasks, calls) · Leads (status, source) · Accounts — all filtered to your MA team.
Connect Your Org
Credentials are used only in your browser — never sent anywhere except Salesforce.
